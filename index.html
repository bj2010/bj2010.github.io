Professor.Dr.Jing Bai（白静）

Jing Bai, professor and master candidate supervisor, is engaged in the research work in CAD&CG and machine learning and plungesherself into the study of 3D model retrieval and deep learning. In the last five years, she has presided over 2 projects funded by National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), 6 provincial and ministerial scientific research projects and 5 national projects, published more than 40 academic papers in domestic and foreign top journals, such as Computer-Aided Design, Journal of Computer-Aided Design and Computer Graphics, and important international academic conferences like ECCV, including 10 papers retrievableby SCI, 29 papers included in EI, and 1 paper for CCF Tier-A and Tier-B conferences each, and applied for 12 patents for invention.
